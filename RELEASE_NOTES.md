### Overview

v1.0 is the first intentional release of the personal site — a complete departure from the earlier template-driven blog. This version is a single-page, statically generated presence built to reflect who Amir is today: a problem solver first, an AI Solutions Architect second, and the creator of **The Harvest Methodology**.

No filler. No placeholder posts. No private GitHub projects on display. Just identity, operating philosophy, and writing — presented with precision.

---

### Features

- **Single-page architecture** — Hero, About, Methodology, and Writing on one fast, scrollable page
- **Anchor navigation** — Header links to `#about`, `#method`, `#writing`, plus a direct Medium link
- **Harvest Methodology showcase** — Core loop (`Bet → Deploy → Deliver → Evaluate → Harvest → Reuse`) and three pillar cards
- **Medium writing index** — 10 published articles with dates, direct links, and a flagship tag on the anchor piece
- **Impact stats row** — `$1.5M+` B2B contracts, `10x` operational compression, `6+ yrs` shipping production AI
- **Social presence** — Medium, GitHub, X, and email in the footer
- **SEO & sharing** — Page title, meta description, Open Graph, Twitter card, and canonical URL
- **Analytics** — Matomo tracking in production
- **Custom 404** — On-brand “Signal lost” page with a return-home CTA
- **Static deploy** — Astro 5 build → GitHub Pages via GitHub Actions

### Style & Vibe

**Minimal, but not empty.**  
**Futuristic, but not flashy.**  
**Professional, but not corporate.**

The aesthetic is deliberately restrained:

- **Dark-only palette** — Deep near-black (`#05070a`) with cool slate text and a cyan accent (`#38bdf8`)
- **Ambient depth** — Subtle radial glow and a faint technical grid — engineered atmosphere without visual noise
- **Monospace typography** — Preserved from the original site; signals precision, systems thinking, and craft
- **Terminal-inspired labels** — `// ABOUT`, `// WRITING` eyebrows for a builder’s voice
- **Micro-interactions** — Cards lift and glow on hover; links shift to accent cyan; the header carries a pulsing status dot
- **Glowing hero name** — A quiet signal of presence, not spectacle
- **Node favicon** — Replaced the playful emoji with a minimal cyan target mark

The result reads less like a portfolio and more like a **field manual from someone who ships** — calm, sharp, and confident.
